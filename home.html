Welcome to Burnley Art Festival

About Us

Welcome to the Burnley Art Festival, a celebration of creativity, culture, and artistic expression in the heart of Burnley. Our festival brings together talented artists, art enthusiasts, and the local community for an unforgettable experience.

This year, we're proud to showcase a diverse range of artworks, from contemporary pieces to traditional masterpieces. Join us for exhibitions, workshops, and special events that celebrate the vibrant art scene in Burnley.

Whether you're a seasoned art collector or simply curious about the world of art, the Burnley Art Festival offers something for everyone. Explore our galleries, meet the artists, and immerse yourself in the beauty of artistic expression.
