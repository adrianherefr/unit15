Find Us at Scott Park

The Burnley Art Festival is located at Scott Park. Use the map above to find directions to our venue. We're easily accessible by public transport and have parking facilities available for visitors.

Venue Information
Our festival takes place at the beautiful Scott Park, providing a perfect setting for our art exhibitions. The venue features spacious galleries, workshop areas, and comfortable spaces for visitors to enjoy the artwork at their leisure.
